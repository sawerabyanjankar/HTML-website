📖 About Pokhara

Pokhara is one of the most beautiful cities in Nepal. It is famous for its lakes, mountains, peaceful environment, and tourism. Many people visit Pokhara to enjoy nature, adventure sports, and the beautiful view of the Himalayas.

📍 Location

Pokhara is located in Gandaki Province of Nepal. It lies about 200 kilometers west of Kathmandu and is one of the most popular tourist destinations in Nepal.

📜 History

Pokhara has been an important trade route between Nepal and Tibet for many years. Today, it is known as a tourism capital of Nepal because of its natural beauty and peaceful atmosphere.

🏞️ Famous Places in Pokhara

  • Phewa Lake
  • Davis Falls
  • World Peace Pagoda
  • Sarangkot
  • Begnas Lake
