🌄 About Pokhara

Pokhara is Nepal’s second-largest city and one of its most beautiful destinations. Located about 200 km west of Kathmandu, it sits beside the serene Phewa Lake and offers breathtaking views of the Annapurna Mountain Range. It’s known as the gateway to trekking routes like the Annapurna Circuit.

📍 Location Pokhara lies in central Nepal within Gandaki Province. It’s easily accessible and serves as a hub for both adventure and relaxation tourism.
