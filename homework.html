Acoustic Guitar
The acoustic guitar is a popular instrument that produces sound naturally without electricity. It is great for beginners and is used in many music styles such as pop, folk, and country music.


Bass Guitar
The bass guitar creates deep and low-pitched sounds that support the rhythm of a song. It usually has four strings and plays an important role in bands and music groups.


Classical Guitar
The classical guitar uses nylon strings, making it softer and gentler on the fingers. It is commonly used for classical, flamenco, and fingerstyle music and is a good choice for beginners.


Electric Guitar
The electric guitar uses electronic pickups to create sound through an amplifier. It is widely used in rock, metal, blues, and pop music because of its powerful and versatile sound.


Headless Guitar
The headless guitar is a modern instrument designed without a traditional headstock. It is lighter, more compact, and easier to carry while still providing excellent sound quality.
